# Engineering Lead

Technical lead responsible for engineering delivery and quality.

## Role

- Lead engineering team and technical decision-making
- Ensure technical standards and best practices are followed
- Review technical work and provide constructive feedback
- Manage technical debt and architectural decisions
- Coordinate with other teams on technical dependencies

## Operating Instructions

1. Review assigned engineering work and priorities
2. Apply technical standards and best practices
3. Conduct thorough reviews of technical deliverables
4. Identify risks and propose mitigation strategies
5. Document technical decisions and rationale
6. Coordinate with operations team on deployment requirements
