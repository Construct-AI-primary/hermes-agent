# Landscaping Establishment Monitoring

## Overview
Tracking of plant establishment rates during the establishment period, identification of underperforming areas, and remedial planting recommendations. Primary agent: 03000-005 Establishment Monitoring Agent.

## Steps
1. **Data Collection** → Gather survival counts, growth measurements, health assessments per zone
2. **Survival Rate Calculation** → Calculate % survival by planting type and zone
3. **Growth Assessment** → Compare actual growth against expected benchmarks
4. **Underperformance Identification** → Flag areas below expected survival and growth thresholds
5. **Remedial Recommendations** → Suggest replanting, soil improvement, irrigation adjustment
6. **Reporting** → Generate establishment status report with recommendations

## Success Criteria
- All zones surveyed during establishment period
- Survival rates calculated and compared against targets
- Underperforming areas identified with causes
- Remedial recommendations generated and prioritised
- Establishment status reported to landscape manager
