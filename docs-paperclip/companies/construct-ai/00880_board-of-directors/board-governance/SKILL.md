# Board Governance Skill

## Overview

This skill provides capability for board-level governance oversight, including fiduciary monitoring, strategic tracking, compliance verification, and governance report generation. It supports the Board of Directors (00880) in their governance responsibilities.

## Triggers

- Board meeting preparation required
- Fiduciary performance review needed
- Strategic progress monitoring requested
- Governance compliance check required
- Board report generation needed

## Prerequisites

- Access to financial performance data
- Access to strategic plan milestones
- Access to risk register
- Access to compliance register
- Board of Directors domain knowledge loaded

## Steps

### Step 1: Gather Governance Data
- Collect financial metrics from Finance Director
- Collect strategic progress from Projects Director
- Collect risk status from Risk Management
- Collect compliance status from Governance
- Collect HSE metrics from HSE Director

### Step 2: Analyze Fiduciary Position
- Review financial performance against targets
- Analyze capital allocation effectiveness
- Assess ROI and investment performance
- Flag any fiduciary concerns

### Step 3: Assess Strategic Progress
- Track milestone achievement rate
- Identify strategic objectives at risk
- Compare performance against strategic plan
- Highlight variances requiring board attention

### Step 4: Verify Compliance
- Check regulatory compliance status
- Verify governance framework adherence
- Confirm audit completion rate
- Flag non-compliance items

### Step 5: Generate Governance Report
- Compile board briefing document
- Include fiduciary analysis
- Include strategic progress summary
- Include compliance status
- Include recommendations for board action

## Success Criteria

- All governance data sources reconciled
- Fiduciary analysis complete with supporting data
- Strategic progress assessment accurate and current
- Compliance status verified against all requirements
- Board briefing document complete and accurate

## Pitfalls

- Do not present unaudited financial data as final
- Do not downplay governance compliance issues
- Do not include confidential information in general reports
- Do not recommend board actions that require deliberation
