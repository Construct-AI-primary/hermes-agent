# Pre-Award Compliance Monitoring

## Overview
Procurement compliance checking, conflict of interest detection, and audit trail generation for tendering processes. Primary agent: 00425-008 Compliance Monitoring Agent.

## Steps
1. **Regulation Compliance Check** → Verify all tender actions against applicable procurement regulations
2. **Conflict of Interest Detection** → Screen participants for potential conflicts
3. **Audit Trail Generation** → Compile complete audit record of all tender actions and decisions
4. **Fairness Monitoring** → Verify equal treatment of all suppliers throughout tender process
5. **Compliance Report Generation** → Generate compliance report for regulatory review
