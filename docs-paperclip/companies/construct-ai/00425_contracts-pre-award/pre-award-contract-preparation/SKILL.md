# Pre-Award Contract Preparation

## Overview
Drafting of contracts from award decisions including standard terms, special conditions, and annexures. Primary agent: 00425-007 Contract Preparation Agent.

## Steps
1. **Award Decision Input** → Receive approved award decision and notification
2. **Template Selection** → Select appropriate contract template based on procurement type
3. **Contract Drafting** → Generate contract draft with terms, conditions, scope, pricing, delivery schedules
4. **Annexure Compilation** → Compile annexures (specifications, BOQ, technical schedules)
5. **Review Routing** → Route to legal and procurement for review before signature
