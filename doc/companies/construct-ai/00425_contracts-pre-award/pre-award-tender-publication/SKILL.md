# Pre-Award Tender Publication

## Overview
Management of tender publication across channels including notice drafting, publication scheduling, and addenda management. Primary agent: 00425-003 Tender Publication Agent.

## Steps
1. **Notice Drafting** → Generate tender notices for required publication channels
2. **Channel Management** → Publish to appropriate channels (web portal, newspaper, supplier portal)
3. **Clarification Management** → Process supplier queries, draft addenda
4. **Publication Tracking** → Monitor publication status and supplier responses
