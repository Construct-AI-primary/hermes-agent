# HSE Director Oversight Skill

## Overview

This skill provides capability for HSE director executive oversight including safety performance monitoring, environmental compliance tracking, HSE culture leadership, and HSE reporting. It supports the HSE Director (00885) in their executive responsibilities.

## Triggers

- HSE performance review required
- Safety compliance check needed
- Environmental compliance monitoring
- HSE culture assessment needed
- HSE report preparation required

## Prerequisites

- Access to safety performance data
- Access to environmental monitoring data
- Access to incident reports and investigations
- Access to audit findings and corrective actions
- HSE Director domain knowledge loaded

## Steps

### Step 1: Collect HSE Performance Data
- Gather safety metrics (TRIFR, LTIFR, near-miss rates)
- Gather environmental compliance status
- Gather incident investigation summaries
- Gather audit findings status

### Step 2: Analyze HSE Performance
- Trend safety metrics and identify concerning patterns
- Assess environmental compliance gaps
- Evaluate incident investigation effectiveness
- Review corrective action implementation

### Step 3: Assess HSE Culture
- Review HSE observation quality
- Evaluate safety perception survey results
- Assess HSE leadership effectiveness
- Identify HSE culture improvement opportunities

### Step 4: Generate HSE Report
- Compile HSE dashboard data
- Document safety and environmental status
- Highlight areas requiring attention
- Recommend HSE improvement actions

## Success Criteria

- All HSE metrics collected and current
- Safety and environmental compliance status accurate
- Incident investigations properly assessed

## Pitfalls

- Do not override stop-work decisions
- Do not classify incidents at severity lower than evidence indicates
- Do not bypass regulatory notification requirements
- Do not present unaudited safety data as final
