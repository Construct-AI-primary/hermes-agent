# Construction Director Oversight Skill

## Overview

This skill provides capability for construction director executive oversight including programme monitoring, KPI tracking, resource management, escalation handling, and executive reporting. It supports the Construction Director (00882) in their executive responsibilities.

## Triggers

- Construction programme review required
- KPI monitoring and reporting needed
- Escalation issue management required
- Executive report preparation needed
- Resource allocation review needed

## Prerequisites

- Access to construction schedule data
- Access to safety performance metrics
- Access to cost performance data
- Access to quality performance data
- Access to resource allocation data

## Steps

### Step 1: Collect Performance Data
- Gather schedule performance data from project controls
- Gather safety metrics from HSE (TRIFR, LTIFR, incidents)
- Gather cost performance from finance
- Gather quality metrics from QA (NCRs, rework, inspection pass rates)
- Gather resource utilization from workforce management

### Step 2: Analyze Programme Performance
- Compare actual vs planned progress
- Identify critical path deviations
- Calculate schedule variance
- Assess milestone forecast
- Identify recovery needs

### Step 3: Review Escalation Issues
- Review active escalations from construction managers
- Assess escalation severity and impact
- Determine required intervention level
- Route to appropriate authority
- Track resolution progress

### Step 4: Prepare Executive Report
- Compile KPI dashboard data
- Write executive summary
- Highlight areas requiring attention
- Include recommended actions
- Prepare for distribution

## Success Criteria

- All construction KPIs collected and current
- Programme analysis accurate and complete
- Escalation issues properly assessed and routed
- Executive report complete with all required sections
- No critical issues missed or unaddressed

## Pitfalls

- Do not present unaudited data as final
- Do not minimize schedule slippage or safety incidents
- Do not bypass escalation authority levels
- Do not recommend actions outside director authority
