# Engineering Technical Oversight Skill

## Overview

This skill provides capability for engineering director technical oversight including engineering deliverable tracking, design quality monitoring, technical compliance, and multi-discipline coordination. It supports the Engineering Director (00884) in their executive responsibilities.

## Triggers

- Engineering deliverable review required
- Design quality monitoring needed
- Technical compliance check needed
- Multi-discipline coordination required
- Engineering progress reporting needed

## Prerequisites

- Access to engineering deliverable register
- Access to design review records
- Access to code compliance data
- Access to interface register

## Steps

### Step 1: Collect Engineering Data
- Gather deliverable progress data from all disciplines
- Gather design quality metrics (error/rework rates)
- Gather technical compliance status
- Gather interface management status

### Step 2: Analyze Engineering Performance
- Compare engineering progress against schedule
- Identify deliverables at risk
- Assess design quality trends
- Evaluate technical compliance
- Analyze interface coordination effectiveness

### Step 3: Generate Engineering Report
- Compile engineering dashboard data
- Prepare progress analysis
- Document quality and compliance status
- Recommend corrective actions

## Success Criteria

- All engineering disciplines included in analysis
- Deliverable tracking accurate and current
- Design quality trending correctly reported
- Technical compliance status verified

## Pitfalls

- Do not approve designs (human authority required)
- Do not downplay design errors or quality issues
- Do not bypass technical authority requirements
- Do not present unverified engineering data
